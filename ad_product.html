About Advaiya

For more than ten years now, Advaiya has been successfully serving large and small organizations, leading technology companies and innovative start-ups by creating marketing programs that stick and technology solutions that build value. As a technology company with focus on strategy and effective execution, we bring together a range of business, technology, marketing and creative capabilities. We are partners with Microsoft (Gold partner), Amazon Web Services, IdeaScale, ADtelligence, Madai, and DataSift.

Our business and marketing capabilities include technology marketing, sales and marketing enablement, economic value assessments, etc. We have expertise in delivering solutions related to enterprise collaboration, enterprise project management, mobile, cloud, Big Data and Business Intelligence, web development and content management, and complex customization and integration. We work in diverse range of technologies and platforms including Microsoft SharePoint, Microsoft Project Server, Windows Azure, Amazon Web Services, Google App Engine, Office 365, iOS, Windows, Android, and more. Our creative capabilities include technical and copy writing, instructional and information design, technology and business documentation and presentations, videos, print design, interactive media, infographics, etc.



We have a new visual identity! As we adopt an identity that is more modern, elegant and vibrant, we restate our commitment to build value for our customers in an innovative, yet disciplined manner. At the same time we celebrate the progress that our services, offerings and capabilities are testimonies of. We are inspired by the immense potential that we have at the leading edge of technology, marketing and business. We are challenging ourselves to be more and more purposeful and progressive, contemporary and relevant.
